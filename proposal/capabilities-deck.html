R2 — Restocking recommendations

Data-driven purchase orders,
operator-controlled budget.

Inputs

Current stock levels · Demand forecast (existing API) · Budget ceiling (operator-supplied)

Algorithm

Prioritizes items with lowest stock/demand ratio. Proposes quantities up to budget ceiling. Remaining items ranked for manual review.

Output

Recommendation table: item · quantity · estimated cost · warehouse.

Exportable. Integrated into existing nav with same design language.

Algorithm reviewed with VP Operations at Week 4 before frontend build begins.
