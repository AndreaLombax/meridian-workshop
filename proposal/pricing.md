## Change requests

Items outside the scope boundary defined in the Timeline (§ Scope boundaries) are handled as change requests. Each change request is estimated within 5 business days of identification. No work on out-of-scope items proceeds without written approval. Change requests are billed at **€950/day**, invoiced on completion.

---

## Assumptions

- Fee is denominated in EUR. Exchange rate risk for non-EUR payment is borne by Meridian.
- Travel to Meridian facilities is not anticipated. Remote delivery assumed. On-site sessions (if requested) billed at cost.
- License costs for third-party tools, if any, are passed through at cost with prior approval.
